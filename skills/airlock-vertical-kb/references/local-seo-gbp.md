# Orbit AI — LOCAL SEO + GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE PLAYBOOK

*The local-first foundation, step by step — the fastest-payback growth work for ~80% of Orbit clients
(single-location and service-area businesses). This is the "how" behind `quality-bar.md` §2.5/§2.5b,
and it feeds `local-landing-pages.md` and `aeo-guide.md`.*

---

## The three local ranking levers (Google's map pack)
**Relevance** (does your profile/site match the query), **Distance** (proximity to the searcher), and
**Prominence** (reviews, citations, links, engagement). You can't move distance — so win on
**relevance + prominence.**

## 1. Google Business Profile (the single biggest local lever)
- **Claim + verify.** One true profile per real location. (Service-area businesses can hide the
  address and set service areas.)
- **Primary category = your money service**; add accurate secondary categories. Category choice is one
  of the strongest ranking signals — pick the most specific true match.
- **NAP:** Name, Address, Phone **exactly** as on the site and everywhere else. Consistency matters.
- **Complete everything:** hours (+ holiday hours), services + descriptions, products, attributes,
  service areas, booking/website links, opening date.
- **Photos/video:** real, current, geotagged; refresh regularly.
- **Q&A:** seed real questions + answers; monitor for new ones.
- **Google Posts:** weekly offers/updates/events — an activity + freshness signal.
- **Messaging/booking:** enable if the client can respond fast (ties to the AI receptionist for
  speed-to-lead).

## 2. Reviews engine (prominence + conversion + AEO)
- **Velocity + recency + rating** all matter — a steady stream beats a one-time burst.
- **Ask every happy customer** (SMS/email request flow; QR at point of service). Make it one tap.
- **Respond to all reviews**, good and bad, professionally (signals engagement; defuses negatives).
- **Never fake reviews or gate them** — against Google policy and the honesty line; it backfires.
- Spread beyond Google to vertical directories (also fuels AI answer synthesis).

## 3. NAP + citations
- Consistent NAP across the top aggregators/directories (Apple Maps, Bing Places, Yelp, vertical
  directories, data aggregators). Fix duplicates/inconsistencies — they dilute prominence.
- Build the citation set that matters for the vertical (e.g., Avvo/justia for law, Healthgrades for
  medical, Houzz for remodeling).

## 4. On-site local SEO
- `LocalBusiness` (+ vertical subtype) schema with `geo`, `areaServed`, hours, `sameAs` → GBP.
- City/service-area pages per `local-landing-pages.md` (cities, not ZIP doorway pages).
- City/region in **titles, H1, and body** where true; embedded map + hours; click-to-call.
- Internal-linking silo: Services ↔ Locations ↔ Areas-We-Serve hub.
- Local content: neighborhood guides, local FAQs, local projects/case studies, community involvement.

## 5. Bilingual local (the moat)
- One GBP, but post + answer Q&A in **English and Spanish**; ES on-site location pages + ES reviews
  requests. Capture "[servicio] cerca de mí" demand competitors ignore.

## 6. Speed-to-lead (where local SEO meets the front office)
Ranking gets the call; **answering wins it.** Route every lead to CRM and respond within minutes —
the **24/7 bilingual AI voice receptionist + grounded chat** (`voice-runtime-layer.md`) is the
conversion multiplier a generator's contact form can't touch.

## 7. Measurement
GBP Insights (calls, direction requests, searches), local rank tracking (map pack + organic by city),
**call tracking**, review growth, and CWV. Review monthly; iterate.

## Tier mapping
Gold: GBP optimized + NAP + on-site local basics + review-request flow. Platinum: + city pages +
content + posts cadence + analytics. Iridium: + regional grid + call tracking + receptionist routing.
Rhodium: + multi-location/franchise management + proactive optimization.

## 30 / 60 / 90 cadence
- **0–30:** claim/verify GBP, fix NAP, categories, complete profile, launch review requests, schema +
  1–2 priority city pages.
- **30–60:** posts cadence, citation cleanup, 2–3 more city pages, ES local pages, call tracking.
- **60–90:** review velocity up, local content/FAQs, measure map-pack movement, expand the grid by
  authority. Local compounds in **weeks**; layer content/AEO for the **6–18 month** moat.
