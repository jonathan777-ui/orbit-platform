③ The rules, in plain English

🛡 Setter guarantee

The setter is paid as if the deal closed at full target price — they did their job booking a qualified appointment, so the closer's pricing can't shrink their check.

🎯 Closer accountability

The closer earns on the actual price. Discount below target and the gap to cover the setter's guarantee comes out of the closer's commission — so hold price, or own the cost.

✋ Platform override

If Orbit approves a strategic low price (to win a logo or a referral source), the company absorbs the gap and the closer is made whole. The company takes that loss knowingly — a closer can't do it alone.

🚀 Q1 accelerator

Months 1–3 pay double the standard rate (20%/20%) on the customer's monthly revenue — your big early cash for landing the account.

♻ Lifetime trail

After the trail-down you keep earning every month the agent stays active — and 50% of it for life even if you stop selling, as long as you never poach a customer.

🏛 The floor protects everyone

No deal can be sold below the price floor — it guarantees the company always clears its costs, which is what lets us pay this aggressively up top.
