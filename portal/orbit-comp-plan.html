🔒 Vesting & bad-actor protection

Active → 100%

Full residual every month the agent stays active. Active = closed or set ≥1 new account in the last 90 days, or holds ≥10 active accounts.

Inactive / departed → 50%

Stop selling or leave in good standing and your residual steps to a 50% legacy stream — for life of each account. Most companies zero you out; we don't.

Poach → 0%

The 50% legacy is contingent on a non-solicit covenant. Pull any customer off the platform and all residuals forfeit immediately. Orbit owns the relationship and will recover the client.

Upfront is escrowed 30 days and released after the Deals Desk confirmation call (client knowingly signed, no out-of-scope promises). Refund/churn clawback: <30d 100% · 30–90d 50% · >90d 0%.
