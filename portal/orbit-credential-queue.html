Voice decision — demo vs. production

Demo (create-only): ElevenLabs is not needed. Pre-render the EN/ES greeting + a short call sample with Qwen3-TTS — ~97 ms latency, English + Spanish, 3-second voice cloning, open-source, pennies per run via hosted APIs. Latency doesn't matter when it's pre-rendered. (Its Spanish leans Latin-American — good for FL/TX.)

Production (live two-way calls): switch to ElevenLabs Flash v2.5 (~75 ms + the real-time agent platform: turn-taking, telephony, interruptions). On a live call that orchestration is what sells it — more than raw voice quality, where the Qwen3 gap is small. Start demos on Qwen3, A/B to Eleven when you go live.
